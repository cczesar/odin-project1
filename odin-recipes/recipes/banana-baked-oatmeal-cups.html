banana baked oatmeal cups

Description

These banana baked oatmeal cups are a delicious and healthy breakfast option that you can make ahead of time. They are perfect for busy mornings when you need a quick and nutritious meal on the go. The combination of ripe bananas, oats, and warm spices makes these oatmeal cups a comforting and satisfying treat that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips or nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

  4. Divide mixture evenly between the prepared muffin cups. If desired, sprinkle a few extra sliced almonds and oats over batter in each cup.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges of the oatmeal cups are golden brown, and the cups feel set, 25 to 30 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
