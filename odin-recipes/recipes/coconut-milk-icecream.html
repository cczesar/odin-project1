Coconut Milk Ice Cream

Description

This coconut milk ice cream is a delicious and dairy-free alternative to traditional ice cream. It's creamy, rich, and full of coconut flavor, making it the perfect treat for those who are lactose intolerant or following a vegan diet. You can customize this recipe with your favorite mix-ins, such as chocolate chips, nuts, or fruit, to create your own unique flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
  4. Scoop and enjoy!
