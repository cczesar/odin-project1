Horchata recipe

Description

Horchata is a refreshing and creamy traditional Mexican drink made from rice, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon. It's perfect for cooling off on a hot day and pairs wonderfully with spicy dishes. This recipe is easy to make and brings the authentic taste of horchata to your home.

You must try it MUCH BETTER THAN JAMAICA

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 5 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Place the rice in a bowl and cover with water. Let it sit for at least 3 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the rice and place it in a blender with 2 cups of water. Blend until the rice is finely ground.
  3. Strain the rice mixture into a pitcher, discarding the rice solids.
  4. Stir in the milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and sugar until well combined.
  5. Chill the horchata in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Stir well before serving over ice. Enjoy!
